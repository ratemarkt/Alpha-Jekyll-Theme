Ratemarkt is the next generation hotel trading platform.
Don't miss the future of travel business!

Ratemarkt is intuitively brings you all the great stuff you need for building your own travel business or extend your existing business with plenty of features. It simply works on cloud to let you manage all your business anywhere, anytime within affordable costs.

Batteries Included

Ratemarkt includes whatever you need for running a travel business in a single shot. Once you sign up, you will own all the stuff you're looking for running your business. Supplier integrations, client management, margins, filters, book keeping, reporting and more... all included.

Cloud Based

All your needs packed into a cloud based app to let you access the platform and manage your business anywhere, anytime within affordable costs! You just grown your business or already being a big business? No worries, cloud apps always has room for any scale of business.

Affordable

Thanks to being cloud based, Ratemarkt is very affordable by its various level of subscription plans accompanied with Pay as You Go model. It's always risk free to start with Ratemarkt because you will be able cancel your subscription anytime wihtout any questioning.

Highly Extensible

Whenever your requirements differentiate, you're very welcome to extend Ratemarkt platform with our Connector and Extension infrastructure. You can find Ratemarkt SDK on Github and give a start developing Ratemarkt by consulting our documentation.
