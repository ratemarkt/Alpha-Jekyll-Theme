Ratemarkt is the next generation hotel trading platform.
Don't miss the future of travel business!

Ratemarkt intuitively brings you all the great stuff you need for building your own travel business or extending your existing business with plenty of features. It simply works on cloud to let you manage all your business anywhere, anytime within affordable costs.

Platform Highlights

Batteries Included

Ratemarkt includes everything you need for running a travel business in a single shot. Once you sign up, you will own all the stuff you're looking for running your business. Supplier integrations, client management, payments, margins, filters, book keeping, reporting and more... all included.

Cloud Based

All your needs packed into a cloud based web application to let you access the platform and manage your business anywhere, anytime within affordable costs! You just grown your business or already being a big business? No worries, cloud apps always has room for any scale of business.

Developers First

As Ratemarkt, as being a technology grounded SaaS company, developers are always going to be our first class citizens because we believe that there is no problem on earth which softwware can not solve except miscommunication. So we will always try so hard to improve our communication to them over easy to use SDKs, extensive set of client libraries and comprehensive documentations.

Global Marketplace

We are very proud of as being the first one inventing a real Travel Marketplace which any kind of hotel suppliers and travel agencies world wide can come together and make business for free. It's just a matter of opening a store in marketplace to start selling and just a matter of sending a client request to suppliers to start buying. You think of how payments will occur? Don't worry, Ratemarkt will be providing you a set of payment and remitment solutions for you.

Very Affordable

Thanks to being cloud based, Ratemarkt is very affordable by its various levels of subscription plans accompanied with Pay as You Go subscription model. Ratemarkt will never be doing a commission business among the suppliers and agencies but providing them the technology platform as long as they want to get benefit. And it's always risk free to start with Ratemarkt because you're totally free to cancel your subscription anytime wihtout any questioning.

Highly Extensible

Whenever your requirements differentiate, you're very welcome to extend Ratemarkt platform with our Connector and Extension infrastructure. You can find Ratemarkt SDK on Github and give a start developing Ratemarkt by consulting our documentation.
