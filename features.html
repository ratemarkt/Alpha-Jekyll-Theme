Popular Features

Search & Booking Engine

This is where our engineering skills come into existence and where business confidence comes from. Our well crafted and well designed search and booking engine ensures that every single transaction is being kept secure, fast and error-free.Also thanks to being cloud based, we always welcome any more traffic, more business and more growth by relying on our auto-scaling cloud server infrastructre.

Payment Collection & Remitment

We complement our booking engine with payment collection and remitment engines in order to solve one of the most important pillars of booking process. Ratemarkt enables 100+ payment gateways around the world to ensure none of our merchants miss any single payment. Ratemarkt also integrates with the most popular VAN issuers to let you make payments to your suppliers at the time of booking instantly!

Ready Made Supplier Connectivities

Ratemarkt supports plenty of suppliers integrated into its booking engine to able to bring you your favorite suppliers ready to plug and go without any need of integration effort. Just enter your credentials to the system obtained from the supplier due to your private commercial contract and start receiving bookings instantly!

Client & API Access Management

Ratemarkt let you manage your clients and each client account (API Key) belongs to each client with its intuitive management console. You can easily treat your clients separately by configuring each of them with different set of rules, margins, suppliers, filters and plugins. Are you looking for something to control the amount of API calls of your clients? You can set API access limits for your clients separately according to their efficiency.

Revenue Management

Ratemarkt offers a margin and filtering console to let you maximize your revenue by applying smart price discrimination strategies to each of your client according to the nature of their sales profile. Thanks to our Insights Reporting, you can easily identify your sales performance and competitiveness results of your pricing strategy and regularly review your pricing in order to keep your sales sky rocket.

Marketplace Featured

When Ratemarkt started its journey on being an ultimate technology solution platform for any kind and scale of business, rather than being just a technology solution, we thought that we should also build a marketplace for our customers so that they can trade with each other by removing boundaries of the world of commercial borders and bureaucracies. Ratemarkt today proudly comes you with an ingenious hotel trading platform which is intended to be the ultimate market for all hotel suppliers and travel agencies worldwide

Book Keeping

Ratemarkt always keeps track of all your transactional records of both client side and supplier side so that you can easily review and manage your financial positioning according to your billings and invoices and build a comprehensive archive for your accounting facilities.

Plugins

Requirements may change anytime! Ratemarkt comes with an extensible API infrastructure so that you can always extend Ratemarkt functionality according to your own needs. We provide a Software Development Kit to let our merchants develop their own Plugins to conform their special needs. Please consult to our Developers section for further information.

Insights

Ratemarkt believes in data and works very hard to provide you insightful reports to make you a super hero by empowering you with the most invaluable data hints to let you give the most appropriate data driven decisions for your business.

Social

We are very proud of being the first one bringing social networking bits to our slightly boring business lives. All the Ratemark members has a profile which can be identified by other organisations while doing business in marketplace. Ratemarkt also introduces a gamification for coworkers within an organisations by keeping their sales performance upon the clients they manage.
